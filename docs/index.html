    <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

    

    <h3>How do I use the timer?</h3>

    <p>Simply tap the play button to start your meditation session. You can pause at any time by tapping the pause button.</p>

    

    <h3>Can I set a time limit for my meditation?</h3>

    <p>Yes! Go to settings and enable "Time Limit". You can then set your desired meditation duration.</p>

    

    <h3>How do I save my meditation sessions?</h3>

    <p>Sessions automatically save to Apple Health at the end of a timed session. For open ended sessions or ending timed sessions early, pause and stop the timer then tap the heart icon to save your session to Apple Health.</p>

    

    <h3>Where can I view my meditation history?</h3>

    <p>Sessions are all logged into Apple Health.</p>
