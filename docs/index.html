Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the timer?

Simply tap the play button to start your meditation session. You can pause at any time by tapping the pause button.

Can I set a time limit for my meditation?

Yes! Less Timer offers optional time limits from 1 to 60 minutes. Go to settings to enable and customize your time limit.

Does the app have interval chimes?

Yes, Less Timer offers customizable recurring chimes at 1, 2, 5, 10, or 15-minute intervals to help maintain awareness during longer sessions.

How does the Apple Health integration work?

Less Timer can optionally integrate with Apple HealthKit to record your mindfulness minutes. This feature is completely optional and is the only way the app stores your meditation history. You can enable or disable this in the app settings.

Can I use the app without sounds?

Absolutely! You can toggle sounds on/off in the settings. You can also enable or disable vibration feedback based on your preference.

Does Less Timer collect my data?

No. Less Timer takes your privacy seriously. We don't collect any data, all preferences are stored locally on your device, and we use no third-party analytics. The optional HealthKit integration only writes data to your Health app with your permission.
