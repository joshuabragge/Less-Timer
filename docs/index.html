Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the timer?

Simply tap the play button to start your meditation session. You can pause at any time by tapping the pause button.

Can I set a time limit for my meditation?

Yes! Go to settings and enable "Time Limit". You can then set your desired meditation duration.

How do I save my meditation sessions?

Sessions automatically save to Apple Health at the end of a timed session. For open ended sessions or ending timed sessions early, pause and stop the timer then tap the heart icon to save your session to Apple Health.

Where can I view my meditation history?

Sessions are all logged into Apple Health.
